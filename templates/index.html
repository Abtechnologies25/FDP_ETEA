ABOUT NARAYANAGURU ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Narayana Guru College of Engineering (NGCE), an institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi was established in the year 2002 under the aegis of Dr. G Sidharthan, Former Head, Department of Management studies, Anna University Chennai, and Former Registrar of Kerala University. The campus is located amidst a verdant and lush green landscape in Manjalumoodu, Kanyakumari district. The College has grown from strength to strength in the last 21 years and progressing towards Excellence in Engineering Education, Research and Development. In pursuit of excellence, a quality management system is in place in line with ISO 9001-2000. The college is recognized under 2(f) of the UGC act 1956. Accreditations by NAAC is testimony for the quality higher educational service of the college.

ABOUT AB TECHNOLOGIES

AB Technologies is an organization that primarily deals with developing research concepts and in guiding the students to develop engineering projects with real time applications. AB Technologies conducts Training and Courses in various disciplines like Embedded Systems, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics & Automation, Matlab, PCB, CAD design, Python, Java, VLSI, ANSYS, Web Development, Game Development, and Computer Hardware & Software. AB Technologies is experienced in the preparation of proposals and in the implementation of engineering oriented funded projects. AB Technologies is engaged in the manufacturing of Lab Equipments and is well known for conducting various workshops, value added courses, trainings, and Guest Lecture Programs, National and International conferences in various colleges across India.

TOPICS TO BE COVERED IN THE FDP

Engineering applications are undergoing rapid transformation from traditional design and construction to data-driven, sustainable, connected, and intelligent systems due to advancements in technology, growing environmental concerns, and increasing demand for efficiency and automation. Modern engineering no longer focuses only on design and construction but integrates intelligence, sustainability, and digital connectivity.

This Faculty Development Program on “EMERGING TRENDS IN ENGINEERING APPLICATIONS” focuses on lectures that enable the participants to integrate skills in AI, digital tools, sustainability, and interdisciplinary collaboration to gain knowledge about emerging trends in engineering and to stay future-ready.

TARGET AREAS

  • 2. Internet of Things (IoT) & Connected Engineering
  • 3. Robotics, Intelligent Systems & Automation
  • 4. Sustainable & Green Engineering Solutions – Renewable Energy Systems
